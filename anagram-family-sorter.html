Anagram Family Sorter (Java)

Date Created: February 21, 2021

Course: TCSS 143 - UW Tacoma

Concepts: Comparators, File Reading/Writing, Iterators, LinkedLists, Object Classes

Description: The Anagram Family Sorter takes a list of words from txt file and sorts them into families based on thier anagram form. Families are sorted in alphabetical order while words within each family are sorted in reverse alphabetical order. The 5 largest families, families with 6 words, and the final family are printed to an output file. Through the use of LinkedLists and Iterators, it can sort over 170k words in 2 seconds.
