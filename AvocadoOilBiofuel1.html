Avocado Oil Biofuel #1

By: Dajan Lyttle & Michael Li

Type: Experiment

Status: Completed February 17, 2020

Summary: Created biodiesel with avocado oil and burned it to test fuel value

Details:

  • Transesterified Avocado Oil by mixing with Methanol and a Catalyst
  • Created 2 different samples using 2 catalysts (Potassium Hydroxide (KOH) & Sodium Hydroxide (NaOH))
