Background

This project started as my senior project at the University of Washington Tacoma. I originally worked in a group of four students advised by Dr. Thillainathan Logenthiran. Our goal was to create an algorithm for rerouting power through a microgrid in the event of an emergency. We started work in September 2022 and presented our project in May 2023. After graduation, I continued to worked on the project alone.
